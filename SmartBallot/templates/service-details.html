SmartBallot E-Voting Services

Fully Secured Accessible Anywhere 4.9/5 Rating

SmartBallot provides modern, secure, and transparent voting solutions designed for organizations of all sizes. Whether your election is online, paper-based, or hybrid, we ensure accuracy, integrity, and ease of participation.

Manage Elections

SmartBallot offers a comprehensive, end-to-end election management system designed to streamline every step of the voting process. From setting up ballots to configuring voter eligibility, our platform ensures precision, transparency, and full administrative control. Election managers can monitor real-time statistics, track participation, enforce voting rules, and generate automated results instantly after the voting period ends.

Whether you're running small internal elections or large nationwide polls, SmartBallot provides an intuitive dashboard, role-based permissions, audit trails, and advanced security features that guarantee fairness and accuracy at every stage.

Online Voting

Our secure online voting system allows eligible voters to cast ballots from anywhere, on any device. The platform uses encryption, unique voter tokens, and time-restricted access to prevent impersonation or repeated voting. SmartBallot simplifies the voting experience with a clean and user-friendly interface that guides voters step-by-step from login to confirmation.

Online voting drastically reduces costs associated with manual elections while increasing accessibility for remote members, staff, students, or citizens. Every vote cast is verified, encrypted, and stored safely to ensure the highest level of integrity.

Dedicated Online Voting Website

An exclusive voting website for your elections, branded with your logo and colors. Easy-to-use, cross-platform, accessible and professional.

Flexible Voter Authentication

Voting passwords are generated for each eligible voter and distributed by email or postal letter. Or, Simply Voting integrates with your existing authentication method for a single sign-on solution.

Flexible Ballot Questions

The online voting system supports many types of voting rules and ballot questions including multiple choice, write-ins, and preferential. Attach photos and descriptions to each candidate and add comment boxes to collect feedback from voters.

Overlapping Elections

You can run several elections independently of each other, even simultaneously. If someone is eligible to vote in multiple ballots they will be given the opportunity to vote in each one.

Paper Ballot

For organizations that prefer traditional voting, SmartBallot supports paper-based elections with full logistical and administrative planning. We provide printable ballots, voter lists, ballot tracking, and manual or scanner-assisted counting options. Each ballot is validated using strict anti-tampering procedures to ensure vote authenticity.

Hybrid voting is also supported â€” allowing paper votes and online votes to be combined into a single verified results report without compromising fairness or transparency.

Benefits and Features

In-Person Voting

Allows traditional casting of paper ballots in a ballot box,
simultaneously with online voting.

Mail-In Voting

Support for efficiently processing mail-in ballots, simultaneously with online voting.

One Vote Per Voter

It is impossible to vote twice, even if you try casting internet and telephone ballots at the same time.

Telephone Voting

Telephone voting gives participants a secure alternative when internet access is limited. Voters receive a unique PIN and call a dedicated voting line where an automated system or operator guides them through ballot selection. Each vote is instantly recorded and synchronized with the main election database.

This service is ideal for organizations with older members, remote workers, or accessibility needs. SmartBallot ensures complete confidentiality and prevents duplicate voting across all channels.

No Internet Required

An advantage of telephone voting is that it does not require a computer or internet to vote. Any touch-tone phone will do.

Toll-Free Numbers

Simply Voting can assign a local phone number for voters to call, a toll-free phone number.

Flexible Voting Rules

Most features of internet voting are supported by telephone voting system, including many types of voting rules and ballot questions, segmentation, and vote weight.

Anonymous & Secure

Votes are anonymous. Election administrators and fellow voters will never know how a voter has voted. The voting system is designed around security from the ground-up. Learn more on our Security page.

Accessible Ballots

The voting interface is regularly audited against section 508 accessibility requirements. This ensures voting websites are compatible with screen-reading technology such as JAWS.
